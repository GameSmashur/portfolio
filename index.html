Data management: Policy Administrator

During my time as policy admin on the data management team, my job was to write all policy on how and why things would get done. Our project was to take all website data and find a way to collect it and display it with limitations on who sees what. Because of this experience I learned the basics of google analytics and I learned about writing policy.

Information Security: Network and System Administrator

While I was Network admin on the info sec team, my job was to maintain anything that had to do with our side of the network running smoothly. As system admin i had to manage servers, load-balancers, and data storage. Our project was to impliment a password management system for the company. This experience made me learn about pseudocode and how to plan out an app

System Development: Poduct Owner

While I was the Product Owner of the System Development team, I was responsible for collaboration and owning the delivery of business driven requests. I was also in charge of planning out how we would get the project done throughout a 2 week period. Our project was to completely overhaul the company website with absolute creative freedom. This experience made me take on a very leadership-based role and learn how to be a good leader.
